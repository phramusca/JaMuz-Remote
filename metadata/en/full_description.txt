Audio Player:
 - Playlist editor (user tags, rating and genre)
 - Tag editor (user tags, rating and genre)
 - Replaygain support (MP3 only, FLAC on its way)
 - One finger control
 - Voice commands

Merge your statistics with <a href="https://phramusca.github.io/JaMuz/">JaMuz Desktop</a>
 - And so with all players JaMuz Desktop can merge with (Mixxx, Kodi, guayadeque,...)

Sync files from <a href="https://phramusca.github.io/JaMuz/">JaMuz Desktop</a> over WiFi
 - Select a playlist on JaMuz Desktop, connect JaMuz Android, and wait for your files.

Remote Control for <a href="https://phramusca.github.io/JaMuz/">JaMuz Desktop</a>
